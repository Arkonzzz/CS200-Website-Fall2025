Training Programs

Upper/Lower Split

4 days per week. Heavy compounds + accessories.

  • Upper: Bench, Row, OHP, Pullups
  • Lower: Squat or Deadlift focus

Push/Pull/Legs

6 days per week. Moderate volume; great for intermediates.

  • Push: Bench variations + delts + triceps
  • Pull: Rows + pulldowns + biceps
  • Legs: Squat, RDL, calves

Full-Body 3x/week

Efficient and balanced. Great for busy schedules.

  • Day A: Squat, Bench, Row
  • Day B: Deadlift, OHP, Pullups
