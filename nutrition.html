Nutrition Basics

Fuel your training with consistent meals, sufficient protein, and high-quality carbs and fats.

Tips for Nutrition

  • Start with protein ~0.7-1.0 g per lb bodyweight.
  • Distribute remaining calories across carbs and fats to preference.
  • Track weekly bodyweight trend and adjust ±250 kcal as needed.
